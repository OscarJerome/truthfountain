Our Ministries

Empowering the vulnerable and transforming lives through our dedicated programs.

Children of the Nations Uganda (COTN)

Founded in 2006, this ministry supports orphans and destitute children affected by the insurgency in Northern Uganda. Over 723 children are being cared for.

Truth Vocational and Technical Institute

Founded in 2013, this institute offers practical skills training to school dropouts in areas like carpentry, tailoring, and mechanics.

Crown of Glory Ministry

Distributes meals and hygiene packs to the most vulnerable members of the community, including lepers and the elderly, and provides wheelchairs to those with disabilities.

Eye Camp

The eye camp provided free eye care to 1,400 individuals, offering eye exams and distributing 1,000 pairs of glasses to those in need.

Truth Fountain My Sister's Keeper (MSK) Project

Dedicated to empowering former sex workers and child mothers, this program offers counseling, skills training, and spiritual support, helping them rebuild their lives.
